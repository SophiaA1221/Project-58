Reciting Poems/Speeches

Reciting poems is not about what you recite, but how you recite it. It’s no exception for me. I have memorized and recited many poems and speeches. That’s my favorite hobby. I like doing these things because you almost feel what the poet or writer is feeling. If the speech is sad, you feel sad. If the speech is empty, then you feel empty, et cetera.
